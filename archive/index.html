Body

As the technology leader and partner of choice, the San Francisco Department of Technology aims to improve government services, the community, and the lives of employees, residents, businesses and visitors of the City and County of San Francisco through smart and responsive use of technology.

Initiatives

Introduction to the goals of the initiatives and the Department of Technology’s role.

Connectivity

Expanding pervasive, high-speed broadband Internet connectivity

Digital Service

Modernizing government through digital service delivery

Technology-as-a-Service

Establishing best-in-class tools, methods and experiences for customers and modernizing infrastructure services

Tech Talent

Recruiting, retaining and advancing civic technologists

Cybersecurity

Adopting cybersecurity policy and framework to protect critical systems, data, and infrastructure

San Francisco Wins A Best of the Web Award

September 01, 2016

San Francisco, CA —Today, The Center for Digital Government recognizes the City and County of San Francisco’s Department of Technology with a Best of the Web Award. The department earned the award for its approach to migrating 115 websites in the sfgov.org collection of sites; integrating open-source solutions, to better serve its residents, businesses and visitors.

San Francisco Celebrates World Wi-Fi Day

August 30, 2016

San Francisco, CA —Today, the Department of Technology announced 16 new access points added to the #SFWiFi network. The upgrades are intended to increase the network’s capacity to support greater density during large-scale events along Market Street, allowing more users to connect to the network simultaneously.
