Services

As the technology leader and partner of choice, SFDT offers technology services and solutions to CCSF departments and agencies. We aim to improve government services, the community, and the lives of employees, residents, businesses and visitors of the City and County of San Francisco with smart and responsive technology.

Web Content Management

Manages web content and redesign services for City departments, leveraging usability and web accessibility best practices and principles

Client Services Project Management Office

Provides oversight and management of customer service and client engagement, IT service management and automation, support services, and IT project delivery activities and reporting.

SFGovTV

Produces award-winning media and original programming: live coverage of Board of Supervisor meetings and City Commissions, announcements from the Mayor and a wide variety of city events

Digital Service & Products

Builds modern, sustainable digital products to better serve San Francisco residents, businesses and visitors

Cybersecurity

Provides guidance to strengthen cybersecurity policy and maintains security of City IT infrastructure while defending information resources and responding to active threats

Service Delivery and Technology Operations

Focuses on the delivery of services and systems including technology and infrastructure as a service, software-defined data centers, cloud services, application development and infrastructure engineering.

How can we assist you?

Need to submit an order, make a service request, report an incident? We're here to help.
