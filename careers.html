Benefits

A diverse and inclusive workplace

We pride ourselves in our workforce diversity. The Department of Technology is well above the national average and the technology industry in diversity.

Impact & purpose

Work on exciting, innovative projects that impact the residents and visitors of San Francisco

Learning & development opportunities

Dedicated departmental budget for training, certifications and conferences available to all staff members. Additional training and education reimbursement benefits through local unions.

Comprehensive healthcare options

Health, vision and dental insurance benefits available for all staff, including temporary workers

Flexible vacation policy & paid family leave

Employees can earn paid vacation and sick leave days. All employees receive 11 Federal Holidays and 4-5 floating holidays per year.

Competitive Retirement Benefits

We offer a retirement pension program that provides financial security and peace of mind when you reach retirement age. If you leave the City before you are eligible for retirement, you get it back on your way out.

Discounts from many city retailers and services

Gym memberships, car share memberships, cellular networks...
