About Digital Services and Products

The Digital Service and Products team builds modern, sustainable digital products to better serve San Francisco residents, businesses, and visitors.

The Digital Service and Products team leverages digital assets and tools to modernize “brick-and-mortar” civic service delivery, and offer online alternatives for a constituency that is increasingly expecting “an app for that” for their service needs.

Focusing on alignment of our current resources, the Digital Service and Products team will deliver digital products that are best in the industry. The team has made significant improvements in this area with initiatives such as the San Francisco Business Portal, Housing Portal, and the Office of Short Term Rentals by leveraging our successful “Skunkworks” approach. The Digital Service and Products team will focus on expanding these and similar efforts through a thoughtful staffing plan, close coordination with the City’s constituency to understand their needs, and rapid development of new products and iterations of existing ones.

Core functions include:

  • Design
  • Engineering
  • Product Management
  • Consultancy

FAQ

What does DT Digital Service & Products do?
We work with City departments to build modern, sustainable, public-facing digital products. An example is The San Francisco Business Portal which helps small businesses start, stay in grow in San Francisco. Our core functions include design, engineering, product management, and we act as a consultancy to provide best practices and expertise so that Departments can build their own services and products.
How do I work with the DT Digital Service & Products team?
Contact us if you’re a City department looking to build a new digital product, you’re a city interested in learning from us how to provide digital services for the public, or you’re interested in partnering or collaborating with the team.
What services do you offer?
Our core functions include design, engineering, product management, and we act as a consultancy to provide best practices and expertise so that Departments can build their own services and products.
